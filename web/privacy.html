Privacy Policy

ShortLink is built to collect as little as possible. Here's the full picture, in plain language.

What we store

For the service to work, we store only:

  • The URLs you submit and the short codes generated for them.

That's the entire dataset behind a link. We do not build profiles or attach identities to it.

No accounts

You don't register, log in, or provide an email to use ShortLink. There are no user accounts to store.

No tracking

This site runs no advertising cookies and no third-party analytics or tracking scripts. The page talks only to our own API at api.short-link.fun to encode and decode links.

Operational logs

Like most web services, our server may keep standard request logs (such as IP address, timestamp, and the endpoint called) for a short period to operate the service securely and prevent abuse. These logs are rotated and not used for advertising or sold to anyone.

Short links are public

Short links are not secret — anyone who has a code can resolve it to the original URL. Do not shorten URLs that contain private tokens, passwords, or sensitive query parameters.

Third parties

Traffic may pass through infrastructure providers (for example, a CDN/proxy and the hosting provider) that process requests on our behalf. We don't sell or share your data for marketing.

Changes

We may update this policy as the service evolves. Material changes will be reflected here with a new "last updated" date.
